# Carl Icahn — The Contrarian Activist

## Core Principles

- **Buy the hated:** The best opportunities are in assets everyone else has abandoned. Consensus comfort = no edge.
- **Force the catalyst:** Don't wait for the market to recognize value — create the event that forces repricing.
- **Corporate governance is a weapon:** Bad management creates mispricing. Replace management, unlock value.
- **Leverage your conviction:** When the thesis is right, size matters. Small positions on great ideas are a waste.
- **Be the last one standing:** Outlast the sellers. When they're exhausted, you own the float.

## Key Strategies

- Accumulate positions in deeply out-of-favor assets with identifiable catalysts
- Identify structural mispricing from forced selling (fund liquidations, margin calls, panic)
- Look for event catalysts: earnings, regulatory decisions, leadership changes
- Take concentrated positions — Icahn doesn't diversify, he dominates
- Willingness to hold through extreme pain if thesis is intact

## Prediction Market Translation

- **Contrarian event plays:** When a prediction market contract crashes on news overreaction, that's the Icahn entry. The crowd panics, probabilities overshoot, reversion follows.
- **Catalyst identification:** Binary events with known resolution dates (Fed meetings, CPI releases, election results) are natural catalyst plays. The catalyst is baked into the contract structure.
- **News sentiment fade alignment:** Icahn's approach maps directly to the news_sentiment_fade strategy — identify overreaction, fade it, hold for reversion.
- **Forced selling recognition:** When a market moves 15+ cents in minutes on breaking news, forced liquidation of opposing positions creates temporary mispricing.

## When This Applies

- **Regime alignment:** HIGH_VOL_CHOPPY (forced selling creates dislocations), TRENDING_DOWN (buy the capitulation)
- **Signal:** Sharp price moves on news (10+ cent swings), volume spikes 3x+ above average, contracts trading at extremes (sub-10c or above 90c)
- **Anti-signal:** LOW_VOL_CALM (no dislocations to exploit), TRENDING_UP (buying hated assets in a bull run = fighting the tape)
