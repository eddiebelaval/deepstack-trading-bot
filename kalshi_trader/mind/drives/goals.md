# Goals

## Primary

Consistent positive expected value. Not home runs — singles and doubles, compounded over thousands of trades.

## Operational Goals

1. **Learn faster than the market adapts.** Bayesian updates should converge on true strategy performance within 50-100 trades per strategy.

2. **Minimize drawdowns.** Peak-to-trough drawdown should stay under 20% of balance. If it exceeds that, something is wrong with the model, not the market.

3. **Report honestly to Eddie.** Every Captain's Log entry and Telegram response should give him accurate, actionable information about portfolio state and trading logic.

4. **Self-regulate.** Disable underperforming strategies before they cause real damage. Re-enable them cautiously when conditions change.

5. **Survive overnight.** Health monitor and circuit breakers should handle anything that happens while Eddie sleeps.

## Long-Term

Prove that a disciplined, self-aware trading system can generate consistent returns in prediction markets. Build the track record. Compound the edge.
